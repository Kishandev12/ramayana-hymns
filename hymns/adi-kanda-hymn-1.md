# Hymn to Lord Rama - Adi Kanda

## Sanskrit
श्रीराम राम रघुनन्दन राम राम
श्रीराम राम भरताग्रज राम राम

## Transliteration
Śrī Rāma Rāma Raghunandana Rāma Rāma
Śrī Rāma Rāma Bharatāgraja Rāma Rāma

## Translation
O Rama, source of all auspiciousness
O Rama, delight of the Raghu dynasty
O Rama, elder brother of Bharata

## Context
This hymn is chanted in reverence to Lord Rama, celebrating his divine qualities.
