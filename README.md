## ğŸ“œ About the Ramayana

The Ramayana is one of the two major Sanskrit epics of ancient India, composed by the sage Valmiki. It narrates the life of Rama, a legendary prince of Ayodhya, and is considered both a literary masterpiece and a spiritual text.

### The Seven Kandas
1. **Bala Kanda** - The childhood and early life of Rama
2. **Ayodhya Kanda** - Events in Ayodhya leading to exile
3. **Aranya Kanda** - Life in the forest and Sita's abduction
4. **Kishkinda Kanda** - Alliance with Sugriva and Hanuman
5. **Sundara Kanda** - Hanuman's journey to Lanka
6. **Yuddha Kanda** - The great war with Ravana
7. **Uttara Kanda** - Return to Ayodhya and aftermath
